Three years ago, I started writing a story called Skylight. The story takes place in another solar system where society never looked up and wondered about the stars, until one day, they did. If I ever publish this, I might use this skylight optical illusion from Keith Weston as the cover art. Do you see the pyramid?

Over the past year, my attention was diverted to another project, but shelter-in-place has given me some free time to finish Chapters 5 and 6. For context, I've posted Chapters 1 & 2 and Chapters 3 & 4 previously.

Hope you like it. I'll try to get 7 and 8 out a lot quicker.
