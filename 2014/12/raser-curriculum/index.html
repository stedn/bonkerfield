RASER Curriculum

2014·12·30 | will stedden

I'm running the Ray After School Electronics and Robotics (RASER) Program at Ray Elementary this Winter. The program is going to culminate with a competition on building an Arduino based security system. And each week before that will have a session on one of the sub-components of the project.

Here are the project titles, and I'll update with links to descriptions as I build them.

  • Week 1: LED circuits
  • Week 2: LED dimmers and switches
  • Week 3: Arduino LED blinker
  • Week 4: Arduino LED Passcode Pad - Part 1
  • Week 5: Review Game
  • Week 6: Arduino LED Passcode Pad - Part 2
  • Week 7: Review LED Passcode pad
  • Week 8: Arduino trip wire
  • Week 9: Laser activated trip switch
  • Week 10: Arduino Body heat detector
  • Week 11: Arduino security competition
  • Week 12: Arduino security competition
  • Week 13: Arduino security competition
